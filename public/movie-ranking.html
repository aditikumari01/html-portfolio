The Best Movies According to Aditi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ajab Prem ki Ghazab Kahani

This is my favourite movie. I love the songs of this movie, It's amazing. The story of this movie is Prem falls in love with Jenny, a woman who is already in love with a man named Rahul. Despite this, he agrees to help her marry him and even gets into several comical situations as a result.

Welcome

Really cool comedy type movie. Two Dons, Uday and Majnu meet Rajiv, who belongs to a respectable family, and want to fix their sister's wedding with them. However, when Rajiv's uncle Dr.Ghunghroo refuses to the match, a series of hilarious situations occur.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic. It's Ryan Gosling movie where he play a stuntman and getaway driver roll falls in love with Irene who is married to a criminal. In a bid to protect, he decides to cross over to the other side of the law.
